Methodology Overview

How Clavix translates raw market and news data into a daily letter grade for every holding.

01 · DATA COLLECTION

We aggregate price, fundamentals and curated news from Polygon, Finnhub, Google News RSS and CNBC.

02 · RELEVANCE FILTERING

Articles are deduplicated by canonical URL and filtered for ticker relevance before scoring.

03 · RISK ANALYSIS

Each holding is scored across four dimensions: news sentiment, macro exposure, sector exposure, volatility.

⚠ Says four dimensions — CLAVIX_TRUTH §6 mandates five (Financial Health is missing here).
04 · GRADE ASSIGNMENT

Composite scores are mapped to a bond-rating-style letter (AAA → F) using fixed 10-point bands.
