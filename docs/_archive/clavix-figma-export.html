Methodology
Methodology Overview
Clavix evaluates holdings across multiple risk dimensions including market structure, macro sensitivity, news risk signals, and catalyst quality.
01
Data Collection
Real-time price, news, and market structure signals are gathered for each holding.
02
Relevance Filtering
Market noise is filtered out so only holding-relevant stories move forward.
03
Risk Analysis
Each holding is scored across four dimensions: news risk signals, macro exposure, position sizing, and volatility trend.
04
Grade Assignment
Composite scores are mapped to letter grades with fixed boundaries.
All scores are informational model outputs only. They do not constitute financial advice and should not be used as the sole basis for investment decisions.
