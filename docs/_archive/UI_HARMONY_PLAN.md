# UI Harmony Plan

## Observe
- Review the app on 3 device sizes: small iPhone, current iPhone, larger iPhone.
- Capture login, onboarding, holdings, ticker detail, digest, alerts, and settings screens.
- Check for scroll during typing, oversized primary actions, cramped headers, and unreadable secondary text.

## Fix
- Keep typing screens fixed-height and keyboard-safe.
- Reduce auth button density and keep button shapes consistent.
- Rebuild ticker detail around the score, the four risk bars, and the supporting cards.
- Increase text size only where readability is weak on small screens.
- Keep edge radii, padding, and card hierarchy consistent across all tabs.

## Accept
- No login/onboarding scroll while typing.
- Risk-dimension bars are visible on ticker detail.
- Primary actions feel balanced, not oversized.
- Small-screen screenshots are readable without layout breakage.
