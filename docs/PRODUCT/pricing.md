# Clavix Pricing

## Status: LIVE — $20/mo Pro tier

Last updated: April 12, 2026

---

## Pricing Model

### Free Tier
- Up to 5 holdings
- Daily digest
- A–F grade per position
- Top news per holding
- Grade change alerts

### Pro — $20/month or $99/year
- Unlimited holdings
- Full score breakdown by dimension
- Position detail with methodology
- MiroFish deep analysis on major events
- Alert history and event log
- Priority analysis during high-volume news days

## Unit Economics

**Break-even:** ~56 users at 20% conversion rate

Profit formula:
```
profit = (price × conversion × total_users) - 111 - (2 × total_users)
```

**At $20/mo, 20% conversion:**
- 50 users: -$11/mo
- 100 users: +$89/mo
- 200 users: +$289/mo

**At $20/mo, 15% conversion:**
- 100 users: -$11/mo
- 200 users: +$89/mo
