Clavix tracks macro conditions, sector exposure, company-specific news, financial health, and volatility. Those inputs roll into five risk dimensions and a composite bond-style grade for each tracked position.
Each ticker is scored across five dimensions: Financial Health, News Score, Macro Exposure, Sector Exposure, and Volatility. Each dimension is weighted equally at 20 percent. If a dimension has limited data, Clavix labels that clearly and recalculates from the available dimensions.
