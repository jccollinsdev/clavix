How it works

Three steps. No broker login.

Clavix works with manually entered positions — no linked accounts, no OAuth. Add tickers, get graded overnight, read your brief in the morning.

Add your holdings

Enter your tickers and quantities manually. No broker login. No linked accounts. Your data stays with you.
